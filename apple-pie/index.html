Apple Pie

This was my grandmother apple pie recipe. I never seen another one quite like this. It always will be my favorite and has won me several first place prizes in local competitions. I hope it becomes one of your favorites as well!!!

Ingredients

- 1 recipe pastry for 9 inch double crust pie

- 1/2 cup unsalted butter

- 3 tablespoon all-purpose flour

- 1/4 cup of water

- 3 tablespoon all-purpose flour

- 1/4 cup of water

- 1/2 cup of white sugar

- 1/2 cup packed brown sugar

- 8 Granny Smith Apples - peeled, cored and sliced

Directions

1) Preheat oven 425 degrees F(220 degrees C). Melt the butter in a saucepan. Stir in flour to form a paste. Add water, white sugar and brown sugar , and bring to boil. Reduce temperature and let simmer.

2) Place the bottom crust in your pan.Fill the apples, mounded slightly. Cover with a lattice work crust. Gently pour the sugar and butter liquid over the crust. Pour slowly so that it does not run off.

3)Bake 15 minutes in the preheated oven. Reduce the temperature to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Continue baking for 35 to 45 minutes, until apples are soft.
